# am - to love

# fin (Latin: “end” or “boundary”)

definitive a.权威的，确定的

trajectory (trans - across) n.轨道，轨迹

# duc/duct (Latin: ducere, “to lead”)

seduction n.引诱，勾引 （尤指性方面）

sequential a.连续的
